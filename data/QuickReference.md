# 🚀 PixelForge Quick Reference

Welcome to the **PixelForge** command deck. Power up your workflow with these essential shortcuts.

---

## 🎨 Sprite Editor & Tools

### 🖌️ Drawing & Ink
*   **Draw**: `B` (Pencil), `Shift+B` (Spray)
*   **Eraser**: `E`
*   **Eyedropper**: `I`
*   **Pick Colors**: `Alt` (Foreground), `Ctrl+Alt` (Background)
*   **Swap FG/BG**: `X`
*   **Brush Size**: `+` / `-` or `Ctrl + Scroll`
*   **Shading Mode**: `Ctrl` (Hold while using Pencil)

### ✨ Specialized Tools
*   **Rect Marquee**: `M`
*   **Lasso**: `Q`
*   **Magic Wand**: `W`
*   **Hand (Pan)**: `H` or `Space + Click`
*   **Zoom**: `Z` or `Ctrl + Scroll`
*   **Paint Bucket**: `G` | **Gradient**: `Shift+G`
*   **Line**: `L` | **Curve**: `Shift+L`
*   **Rectangle**: `U` | **Ellipse**: `Shift+U`

---

## 🎞️ Timeline & Animation (Tab)

### ⏳ Frame Control
*   **Play/Stop Animation**: `Enter`
*   **Next Frame**: `.` (Period)
*   **Previous Frame**: `,` (Comma)
*   **Goto First/Last Frame**: `Home` / `End`
*   **New Frame**: `Alt+N` | **New Empty Frame**: `Alt+B`
*   **Duplicate Cel**: `Alt+D`
*   **Delete Frame**: `Alt+C`

### 🕯️ Onion Skinning
*   **Toggle Onion Skin**: `F3`
*   **Set Loop Section**: `F2`
*   **Onion Skin Settings**: `Ctrl+F3`

---

## 🧩 Layers & Organization

*   **New Layer**: `Shift+N`
*   **New Layer Below**: `Shift+Space+N`
*   **New Group**: `Alt+Shift+N`
*   **Layer Properties**: `Shift+P`
*   **Select Next Layer**: `Up Arrow`
*   **Select Prev Layer**: `Down Arrow`
*   **Hide/Show Layer**: `Shift+X`

---

## 🖼️ View & Workspace

*   **Toggle Timeline**: `Tab`
*   **Palette Editor**: `F4`
*   **Preview Window**: `F7`
*   **Fullscreen Preview**: `F8`
*   **Show Grid**: `Ctrl + '`
*   **Snap to Grid**: `Shift + S`
*   **Show Extras**: `Ctrl + H`
*   **Advanced Mode**: `Ctrl + F`

---

## 🛠️ Selection & Transformation

*   **Select All**: `Ctrl+A`
*   **Deselect**: `Ctrl+D`
*   **Invert Selection**: `Ctrl+Shift+I`
*   **Flip Horizontal**: `Shift+H`
*   **Flip Vertical**: `Shift+V`
*   **Rotate**: `Space + R` (while scaling)
*   **Scale from Center**: `Alt` (Hold)

---

## ⌨️ Customization
*Go to **Edit > Keyboard Shortcuts** to redefine any of these commands.*
